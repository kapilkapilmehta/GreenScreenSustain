The innovation

A prototype concept built around layered, lower-plastic PPE design

The materials concept shown in the supplied deck describes a three-layer construction with an outer paper layer, an inner soap lining, and an innermost cotton lining.

  • Designed from a sustainability-first perspective
  • Developed from frontline medical experience
  • Explored through multiple gown, cap, and full-set prototypes

Founder

Medical insight shaped by decades of ophthalmic practice and public service

Founder Dr. Anjali Mehta is an ophthalmologist, former Army Medical Corps officer, private practitioner, and social impact leader based in Delhi.

Her background includes more than two decades in general ophthalmology, extensive cataract and oculoplasty experience, academic presentations, and community eye-care work through Sagar Trust.

In 2023, she began developing GreenScreen Protect, a sustainable PPE innovation that received an Indian patent and innovation recognition at a national ophthalmology conference.
